# Engineering Resume AI Prompt 🚀

This repository contains a specialized AI System Prompt (`skills.md`) designed to transform your standard resume into a highly optimized, ATS-friendly engineering resume. 

The instructions are strictly based on the gold-standard guidelines from the [r/EngineeringResumes Wiki](https://www.reddit.com/r/EngineeringResumes/wiki/index/).

## 🎯 What Does This Do?
When you feed this prompt to an LLM (like Claude 3, ChatGPT, or Gemini), it forces the AI to act as a ruthless, elite engineering resume reviewer. It will actively strip away fluff and format your experience into high-impact, technical bullet points.

**Key rules enforced by this prompt:**
* **1-Page Limit:** Keeps your resume concise and impactful.
* **STAR / XYZ Methods:** Forces bullet points into Situation-Task-Action-Result formats.
* **Strong Action Verbs:** Starts every bullet with past-tense action verbs (e.g., *Architected, Implemented, Optimized*).
* **Zero Fluff:** Removes soft skills, personal pronouns (I, we, my), and subjective adjectives.
* **Clean Formatting:** Enforces plain-text links, correct date alignments, and a single-column-friendly structure.

## 🛠️ How to Use

### For Claude (Recommended)
1. Go to **Claude** and create a new **Project**.
2. Copy the entire content of `skills.md`.
3. Paste it into the **Custom Instructions** section of your Project.
4. Start a new chat within the project, paste your current resume, and simply say: *"Please review and rewrite my resume according to your instructions."*

### For ChatGPT
1. Go to **Settings > Personalization > Custom Instructions**.
2. Paste the content of `skills.md` into the bottom box (*"How would you like ChatGPT to respond?"*).
3. Start a new chat, paste your resume, and ask for a rewrite.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
While this prompt heavily optimizes your resume based on proven engineering standards, always manually review the AI's output. Ensure all technical metrics and project details are 100% accurate to your actual experience.
